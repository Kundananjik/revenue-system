# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members
- Provide and receive feedback professionally

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, trolling, or discriminatory comments
- Personal attacks or insults
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Sexual language, imagery, or unwelcome advances
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Advocating for or encouraging any of the above behaviors

## Project-Specific Guidelines

### Code Reviews

- Be constructive and specific in feedback
- Focus on the code, not the person
- Acknowledge good work
- Explain your reasoning

### Issue Reporting

- Search for duplicates before creating new issues
- Provide clear reproduction steps for bugs
- Be respectful of maintainer time
- Follow issue templates

### Communication

- Use clear, professional language
- Stay on topic in discussions
- Respect people's time and attention
- Ask questions in appropriate channels

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to:

**Contact**: kundananjisimukonda@gmail.com

Include:
- Your contact information
- Names of individuals involved
- Description of the incident
- Any relevant context or evidence
- Whether you want to remain anonymous

### Response Process

1. **Acknowledgment**: Within 48 hours
2. **Investigation**: Review of the incident
3. **Decision**: Determination of appropriate action
4. **Communication**: Response to reporter and involved parties

### Consequences

Violations may result in:

- **Warning**: Private written warning
- **Temporary Ban**: Temporary suspension from project participation
- **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from project spaces
- **Public Statement**: In severe cases, a public statement may be issued

Actions depend on severity and frequency of violations.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- Project repositories and issue trackers
- Pull requests and code reviews
- Project communication channels (Slack, Discord, etc.)
- Project events and meetups
- Representing the project in public spaces
